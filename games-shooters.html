Metro

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet — the train level. The Caspian. The Taiga. Exodus took Artyom out of the tunnels and onto the open road, and the shift was terrifying in the best way. The best train sequence in gaming history, full stop.

Call of Duty

The franchise that defined online shooters for a generation. Split here by the feature that mattered most — Zombies mode.

— with zombies mode —

Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet — the Zombies Easter Egg runs. Mob of the Dead. Der Eisendrache. Kino der Toten. A mode that became its own genre within a genre, with a lore so dense it spawned entire YouTube channels dedicated to nothing else.

TitleYearRatingNotes
World at War ZOMBIES2008★★★★☆Where it all started. Nacht der Untoten, Shi No Numa. The original — janky, iconic, never forgotten.
Black Ops ZOMBIES2010★★★★★Kino der Toten. Five. Ascension. The EE storyline begins. Black Ops Zombies is arguably the best CoD package ever assembled.
Black Ops 2 ZOMBIES2012★★★★★Mob of the Dead. Origins. Die Rise. The Zombies story peaked here. The multiplayer was precision-tuned perfection alongside it.
Black Ops 3 ZOMBIES2015★★★★☆Shadows of Evil, Der Eisendrache, Revelations. Over-stuffed with lore but overflowing with creativity. Specialist system was divisive.
Black Ops 4 ZOMBIES2018★★★☆☆No campaign. Blackout (BR mode) was brilliant. Zombies solid. Multiplayer overcomplicated. A weird, messy package with genuinely great parts.
Black Ops Cold War ZOMBIES2020★★★★☆Firebase Z, Die Maschine. Round-based Zombies revival. Cold War campaign is underrated. Welcome return to form across the board.
