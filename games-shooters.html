// series

Metro

Dmitry Glukhovsky's post-nuclear Moscow, brought to life by 4A Games. Survival horror beneath the ruins of civilization.

Title Year Rating Notes
Metro 2033 2010 ★★★★☆ Claustrophobic tunnel horror. Artyom's first journey through the Moscow metro. Raw, atmospheric, unforgettable.
Metro: Last Light 2013 ★★★★★ The peak of the series. Refined gameplay, richer world, the Dark Ones resolved. The Revolution DLC alone justifies the whole game.
Metro Exodus 2019 ★★★★★ Out of the tunnels, onto the open road. Semi-open world Russia, brutal survival, the best train level in gaming history.

// series

Call of Duty

The franchise that defined online shooters for a generation. Split here by the feature that mattered most — Zombies mode.

— with zombies mode —

TitleYearRatingNotes
World at War ZOMBIES 2008 ★★★★☆ Where it all started. Nacht der Untoten, Shi No Numa. The original Zombies experience — janky, iconic, never forgotten.
Black Ops ZOMBIES 2010 ★★★★★ Kino der Toten. Five. Ascension. The EE storyline begins. Black Ops Zombies is arguably the best CoD package ever made.
Black Ops 2 ZOMBIES 2012 ★★★★★ Tranzit, TranZit, Mob of the Dead, Origins. The Zombies storyline peaked here. Multiplayer was precision-tuned perfection too.
Black Ops 3 ZOMBIES 2015 ★★★★☆ Shadows of Evil, Der Eisendrache, Revelations. Over-stuffed with lore but brimming with creativity. Specialist system was divisive.
Black Ops 4 ZOMBIES 2018 ★★★☆☆ No campaign. Blackout (BR mode) was brilliant. Zombies excellent. Multiplayer overcomplicated. A weird, messy package with great parts.
Black Ops Cold War ZOMBIES 2020 ★★★★☆ Cold War setting done right. Firebase Z, Die Maschine. Round-based Zombies revival after WWII's forgettable outing. Welcome return to form.

— without zombies mode —

TitleYearRatingNotes
CoD 4: Modern Warfare NO ZOMBIES 2007 ★★★★★ The game that changed everything. All Ghillied Up. The Mile High Club. The multiplayer that hooked a generation. An all-time classic.
MW2 NO ZOMBIES 2009 ★★★★★ No Russian. Rust 1v1. The Favela. Possibly the most-played CoD in history. Broken in ways that people loved it for.
MW3 NO ZOMBIES 2011 ★★★☆☆ Solid if unspectacular. Price and Soap's trilogy ending lands. Survival mode was a nice addition. Lived in Black Ops 2's shadow.
Ghosts NO ZOMBIES 2013 ★★☆☆☆ The start of a rough run for IW. Extinction mode was underrated. Federation storyline went nowhere. Riley the dog was the highlight.

BioShock

Rapture and Columbia — the two greatest cities in gaming, both rotting from the inside out. Irrational Games at its peak.

BioShock 2
Rapture — a decade underwater later

BioShock 2

2010

★★★★☆

Unfairly dismissed as a sequel. Playing as a Big Daddy reframes Rapture entirely. Minerva's Den DLC is a masterpiece in its own right.

BioShock Infinite
Columbia — city in the clouds

BioShock Infinite

2013

★★★★★

Booker DeWitt and Elizabeth in the sky-city of Columbia. The ending rearranges your brain. Burial at Sea ties the whole saga together.
